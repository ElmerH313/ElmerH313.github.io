1 · Overview1 · 总览

GuitarMetro is a metronome built for serious practice: every click is rendered into the audio stream at exact sample positions and driven by your sound card's clock, so the beat never drifts or jitters. The app has three tabs: GuitarMetro 是为认真练琴而做的节拍器：每一声 click 都以精确的采样点位置 直接合成进音频流、由声卡时钟驱动，节拍永不漂移、永不抖动。应用共三个页签：

  • Tuner 调音 — reference-tone tuner for guitar / bass / ukulele.吉他 / 贝斯 / 尤克里里参考音调弦。
  • Fixed Tempo 固定速度 — the main metronome page (opens by default).主节拍器页（启动默认页）。
  • Tempo Trainer 变速训练 — automatic speed-ramping practice sessions.自动爬速的变速训练。

Switching tabs always stops playback, so two pages can never sound at once. The in-app guide (☰ → User guide) covers the same ground as this page. 切换页签会自动停止播放，两页绝不会同时出声。应用内也有一份说明（☰ → 使用说明），内容与本页一致。

TAP tempo测速

Tap the TAP button in time with the music. The app averages your most recent taps (up to the last 5) and sets the BPM. If you pause for more than 2 seconds, measurement resets and starts fresh. At least 2 taps are needed. 跟着音乐节奏点按 TAP 键，应用取最近的敲击（最多 5 次）求平均并设为当前 BPM。停顿超过 2 秒自动重新测量；至少需要敲 2 次。

Beat ring节拍环

The outer ring splits into one segment per beat. The current beat glows as it travels; downbeat segments stay lit in pale blue-white. During a count-in, or on the last measure of a trainer stage, the whole ring turns orange. 外圈按拍数切分，当前拍的光斑流转经过，强拍段常亮淡蓝白。预备拍期间、以及变速训练每档的最后一小节，整环转为橙色。

3 · Time signatures & beat accents3 · 拍号与逐拍重音

Open the meter panel from the bottom quick bar. 15 time signatures are available (default 4/4): 从底部快捷栏打开拍号面板，共 15 种拍号（默认 4/4）：

Group分组Time signatures拍号
x/41/4 · 2/4 · 3/4 · 4/4 · 5/4 · 6/4 · 7/4
x/22/2 · 3/2
x/83/8 · 5/8 · 6/8 · 7/8 · 9/8 · 12/8

Compound meters come with musically sensible secondary accents out of the box — e.g. 12/8 accents beats 1 · 4 · 7 · 10, and 7/8 accents 1 · 4 · 6. 复合拍自带符合乐理的次强拍——如 12/8 默认在第 1·4·7·10 拍，7/8 在第 1·4·6 拍。

Per-beat accents (Groove)逐拍重音（律动）

In the meter panel, tap any beat dot to cycle its level: 在拍号面板中点按任意拍点，循环切换该拍的强弱级别： accent → secondary → normal → mute强 → 次强 → 普通 → 静音.

  • Accent — accent timbre at full volume.强拍——重拍音色、全音量。
  • Secondary — accent timbre at 55% volume.次强拍——重拍音色、55% 音量。
  • Normal — weak-beat timbre.普通拍——轻拍音色。
  • Mute (red ×) — silences only that beat; subdivisions keep playing through it.静音（红色 ×）——只静音该拍本身，细分声部照常发声。

"Reset all" restores the meter's default accent map. Changing the time signature also resets accents to that meter's defaults. Your accent map is saved with presets. 「全部恢复默认」还原该拍号的默认重音；切换拍号也会重置为新拍号的默认值。逐拍重音会随预设一起保存。

The quick-bar button shows the current meter, with the subdivision symbol beneath it whenever something other than quarter notes is selected. The ⟲ button in the panel resets to 4/4 · quarter notes (and turns swing off). 快捷栏按钮键面显示当前拍号，选了非四分音符时在下方显示细分符号。面板中的 ⟲ 一键回到 4/4 · 四分音符（同时关闭 Swing）。

5 · Swing5 · 摇摆 Swing

Swing delays every off-beat subdivision click to create shuffle feel. Toggle it at the top of the meter panel; downbeats always stay on the grid. Swing 会推迟每个反拍细分的时值形成摇摆律动。开关在拍号面板顶部；正拍永远保持在网格上。

  • Ratio: 50–75%, default 2/3 (66.7%, the classic shuffle). Magnetic snap at 50% and 2/3.比例：50–75%，默认 2/3（66.7%，经典 Shuffle）；在 50% 与 2/3 处有磁性吸附。
  • Detent chips: Straight 50% · Light 58% · Shuffle 2/3 · Hard 75%.快捷档：平直 50% · 轻摇 58% · 三连 Shuffle 2/3 · 硬摇 75%。
  • Compatibility: swing needs eighth or sixteenth subdivisions. Enabling it on anything else auto-switches to eighths (your previous choice is remembered and restored when swing turns off); while on, incompatible wheel entries grey out.兼容性：Swing 需要八分或十六分细分。在其他细分下开启会自动切到八分（原选择被记住，关闭 Swing 后恢复）；开启期间不兼容的细分项变灰不可选。

6 · Sounds & volume6 · 音效与音量

Three independent voices, each with its own timbre (☰ drawer → Click sounds, with instant preview) and its own volume (quick bar → Volume): 三个独立声部，各自可选音色（☰ 抽屉 → 音效，选择即试听）和独立音量（快捷栏 → 音量）：

Voice声部Plays on发声位置Default timbre默认音色
Downbeat重拍accented beats (secondary accents use this timbre at 55%)强拍（次强拍用同音色的 55% 音量）High beep高音哔
Weak beats轻拍normal beats普通拍Mid beep中音哔
Subdivision细分off-beat subdivision clicks (suppressed where they coincide with a beat)反拍细分（与正拍重叠处不发声）Low beep低音哔

The 5 timbres, all parametrically synthesized (no samples):5 种音色，全部参数化合成（无采样素材）：

  • High beep (1760 Hz) · Mid beep (1175 Hz) · Low beep (880 Hz) — clean sine bursts.高音哔（1760 Hz）·中音哔（1175 Hz）·低音哔（880 Hz）——干净的正弦短音。
  • Woodblock — 760 + 1900 Hz, fast decay.木鱼——760 + 1900 Hz，快速衰减。
  • Tick — noise burst + 2600 Hz.嗒嗒——噪声脉冲 + 2600 Hz。

Volume model音量模型

  • Master volume — stepless, applies globally and instantly (even mid-training).总音量——无级，全局实时生效（训练中也可即时调）。
  • Per-voice volumes — 10 steps each; drag to zero to mute that voice.分声部音量——各 10 格；拖到底即静音该声部。
Don't want an accent at all? Set the Downbeat voice to the same timbre as Weak beats, or drag its volume to zero. 不想要重音？把重拍声部改成与轻拍相同的音色，或把重拍音量拖到 0。

7 · Presets7 · 预设

5 slots per page, independent between the two metronome pages (quick bar → Presets): 每页 5 个槽位，两个节拍器页各自独立（快捷栏 → 预设）：

  • Fixed Tempo preset stores: BPM, time signature, subdivision, swing on/off + ratio, per-beat accent map.固定速度预设保存：BPM、拍号、细分、Swing 开关与比例、逐拍重音。
  • Trainer preset stores: duration, start BPM, target BPM, smoothness.训练预设保存：时长、起始速度、目标速度、平滑度。
  • Row actions: tap a row = load · 💾 = save / overwrite · 🗑 = delete.操作：点行 = 读取 · 💾 = 保存/覆盖 · 🗑 = 删除。

8 · Tempo Trainer8 · 变速训练

The trainer ramps the tempo automatically from a start BPM to a target BPM over a chosen duration — ideal for building speed gradually. 变速训练在设定时长内自动把速度从起始 BPM 爬升到目标 BPM——循序渐进提速的利器。

Parameter参数Range范围Default默认
Duration时长1 – 30 min (1-min steps)（1 分钟步进）5 min
Start / Target BPM起始 / 目标速度20 – 400 (dual wheel or direct typing; the two values push each other so start ≤ target)（双滚轮或直接输入；两值联动推挤，起始 ≤ 目标）60 → 120
Smoothness平滑度stepless 0–1: Stepped / Medium / Smooth无级 0–1：阶梯 / 适中 / 平滑0.5

How stages are computed档位如何计算

  • Smoothness maps to the BPM increment per stage: fully stepped = +10 BPM per stage, fully smooth = +1 BPM per stage (range +1…+10).平滑度决定每档增幅：最阶梯 = 每档 +10 BPM，最平滑 = 每档 +1 BPM（范围 +1…+10）。
  • The total duration is split evenly across stages, then each stage is rounded to whole measures — speed changes always land exactly on a measure boundary.总时长均分到各档后按整小节取整——变速永远精确落在小节线上。
  • Each speed change is preceded by a one-measure count-in at the new tempo (not counted into the duration; skipped seamlessly if count-in is off).每次变速前先以新速度数一小节预备拍（不计入时长；关闭预备拍则无缝衔接）。

During training训练进行中

  • Progress ring dial: the inner ring fills clockwise with overall session progress; the center shows start → target, the current BPM large, and the per-stage increment.进度环表盘：内环顺时针填充整体训练进度；中心显示 起始 → 目标、当前速度大字和每档增幅。
  • Stage line: "Stage 3/12 · 2 left" plus a measure countdown like 5/32; on the last measure of a stage the ring and countdown turn orange.档位行：如「第 3/12 档 · 剩余 2」加小节倒计时 5/32；每档最后一小节整环与倒计时转橙。
  • Locked during a run: time signature, subdivision, presets and the plan itself. Still adjustable: master volume (instant), per-voice volumes and timbres (apply from the next measure).训练中锁定：拍号、细分、预设与训练方案本身。仍可调：总音量（即时）、分声部音量与音色（下一小节头生效）。
  • When time is up it doesn't stop by default: a completion note pops up (auto-dismisses in 5 s) and the metronome holds the final tempo until you stop it manually. Turn off Keep clicking after finish in ☰ to stop right on time instead.时间到默认不停拍：弹出 5 秒自动消失的完成提示，节拍器保持最终速度直到手动停止；在 ☰ 里关闭「练完继续响」则到时即停。

9 · Count-in9 · 预备拍

Default on (☰ drawer → Count-in). Before playback starts — and before every trainer speed change — the app counts one measure at the target tempo with a distinct low click. The big BPM number blinks once per beat and turns orange, and the beat ring turns orange, so you always know it's a count-in and not the real thing. 默认开启（☰ 抽屉 → 预备拍）。开始播放前——以及变速训练每次换速前——先以目标速度用独立的低音咔声数一小节。期间 BPM 大字随拍闪烁并变橙、节拍环整体转橙，一眼可辨是预备拍而非正式节拍。

10 · Beat flash10 · 爆闪

  • Default off (☰ drawer → Beat flash). When on, the LED glow surges at each beat onset and decays over 0.12 s — downbeats at full intensity, other beats at 60%.默认关闭（☰ 抽屉 → 爆闪）。开启后灯光光晕在每拍起点骤亮、0.12 秒内衰减——重拍全亮，其他拍 60%。
  • Sub-option "Flash on downbeat only" (default off) limits the surge to downbeats.子开关「仅重拍爆闪」（默认关）——只在重拍骤亮。
  • No flashing during count-ins.预备拍期间不爆闪。

11 · Tuner11 · 调弦

A reference-tone tuner (it plays notes for you to match by ear — it does not use the microphone). Pick instrument → string count → tuning; tap a peg on the visual headstock to hear that string, modeled with Karplus-Strong plucked-string synthesis per instrument. The tone repeats every 3.5 seconds until you tap again (or press Space to replay). Selections persist across launches. 参考音式调弦（播放标准音让你用耳朵对齐——不使用麦克风拾音）。选乐器 → 弦数 → 调弦类型；在可视化琴头上点弦钮即听该弦参考音，音色按乐器用 Karplus-Strong 拨弦建模合成。参考音每 3.5 秒自动重复，再点一次停止（Space 可重播）。所有选择自动记忆。

  • Reference pitch (A4): 432 / 434 / 436 / 438 / 439 / 440 / 441 / 442 / 443 / 444 / 445 / 446 Hz, default 440. Changing it while a string sounds replays instantly at the new pitch.基准音（A4）：432 / 434 / 436 / 438 / 439 / 440 / 441 / 442 / 443 / 444 / 445 / 446 Hz，默认 440。发声中切换基准音会立即以新音高重弹。
  • Headstock view: one peg per string (low strings left, high right, like a real 3+3 headstock); peg faces show note name + string number; the sounding string glows cyan and pulses white; the info line shows e.g. E2 · 82.4 Hz.琴头视图：一颗弦钮一根弦（低音在左、高音在右，如真实 3+3 琴头）；钮面标注音名+弦号；发声弦青色点亮并白光脉冲；底部信息行显示如 E2 · 82.4 Hz。

12 · Practice timer & stats12 · 练琴计时与统计

The timer runs automatically while the app is open — nothing to press. The LED pill at the left of the quick bar shows this session's time (H:MM); tap it for the stats panel. 打开应用即自动计时，无需任何操作。快捷栏左端的 LED 药丸块显示本次练琴时长（H:MM），点开查看统计面板。

  • Anti-idle: after 88.8 minutes with no activity (and nothing playing) a "Still practicing?" dialog appears; if unanswered for 5 minutes, the idle time is rolled back out of your log and the timer pauses. Any click or keypress resumes it.防挂机：88.8 分钟无操作（且未在播放）会弹出「还在练吗？」；5 分钟内未回应则回滚挂机时长并暂停计时，任何点击/按键自动恢复。
  • Stats panel: This session · Today · This week · This month · Daily average (only days with ≥1 min count) · Streak (consecutive days with ≥1 min).统计面板：本次练琴 · 今天 · 本周 · 本月 · 本月日均（仅计 ≥1 分钟的天）· 连续打卡（连续 ≥1 分钟的天数）。
  • Details page: per-year view with Year total · Days practiced · Best day, plus a full-year daily-minutes curve; ◀ ▶ switches between years that have data.详细页：按年查看——年累计 · 练琴天数 · 单日最长，附全年逐日分钟曲线；◀ ▶ 切换有数据的年份。
  • Your history is safe: practice logs are stored per year, separately from settings — "Reset to defaults" never touches them.历史数据安全：练琴日志按年独立存储，与设置无关——「恢复默认设置」不会清除。

16 · Saving, languages & privacy16 · 保存、语言与隐私

  • Everything auto-saves — BPM, meter, subdivision, swing, accents, sounds, volumes, toggles and trainer parameters are saved as you change them and restored on next launch. No save button anywhere.一切自动保存——BPM、拍号、细分、Swing、重音、音色、音量、各开关与训练参数改动即存，下次启动自动恢复。全应用没有保存按钮。
  • Zero data collection — no analytics, no ads, no accounts, no network requests in normal use. Everything stays on your device. See the full privacy policy.零数据收集——无统计、无广告、无账号，正常使用不发起任何网络请求，一切数据只在你的设备上。详见隐私政策。

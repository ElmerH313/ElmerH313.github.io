GuitarMetro

The LED metronome for serious practice 认真练琴用的 LED 节拍器

Sample-accurate timing driven by your sound card's clock — no software-timer jitter, ever. Wrapped in a neon LED interface with a real fragment-shader glow. 由声卡时钟驱动的采样级节拍精度——彻底告别软件定时器的抖动。 外加真 fragment shader 渲染的霓虹 LED 界面。

  • Tempo trainer: auto-ramping speed with progress ring变速训练：自动分档爬升 + 进度环
  • Built-in tuner: guitar / bass / ukulele, 17 tunings内置调弦：吉他/贝斯/尤克里里，17 种调弦
  • Practice timer with yearly stats & streaks练琴计时：年度曲线与连续打卡
  • 3 independent voices, 15 meters, 8 subdivisions三声部独立音色，15 种拍号、8 种细分

About关于

Elmer.H is a one-person studio. I play guitar, I got annoyed by wobbly metronomes, so I built one that ticks like hardware. Everything here is crafted end-to-end by me — design, code, sound synthesis and the pixels in between. Elmer.H 是一个人的工作室。我弹吉他，受够了会"漂"的节拍器， 于是做了一个像硬件一样稳的。这里的每件作品都由我一人从头打磨——设计、代码、 声音合成，以及中间的每一个像素。

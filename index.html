GuitarMetro

The LED metronome for serious practice 认真练琴用的 LED 节拍器

As a guitarist I live with a metronome — and after trying a pile of metronome apps, something was always missing, or locked behind a subscription. So I built my own and made it free for everyone: sample-accurate timing driven by your sound card's clock, wrapped in a neon LED interface with a real fragment-shader glow. 吉他手离不开节拍器。各种节拍器 App 下了一堆——要么缺功能， 要么想用就得订阅。索性自己做一款，免费开放给大家：由声卡时钟驱动的 采样级节拍精度，配上真 fragment shader 渲染的霓虹 LED 界面。

  • Tempo trainer: auto-ramping speed with progress ring变速训练：自动分档爬升 + 进度环
  • Built-in tuner: guitar / bass / ukulele, 17 tunings内置调弦：吉他/贝斯/尤克里里，17 种调弦
  • Practice timer with yearly stats & streaks练琴计时：年度曲线与连续打卡
  • 3 independent voices, 15 meters, 8 subdivisions三声部独立音色，15 种拍号、8 种细分
  • 12 languages · Windows / macOS / iOS / Android12 语言 · Windows / macOS / iOS / Android 全平台
